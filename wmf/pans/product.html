Frequently Asked Questions

For high-heat searing, choose an uncoated stainless steel or honeycomb hybrid pan. Their multi-layer bases hold and distribute intense heat evenly, producing the golden, crispy crust that defines a great steak — and both are metal-utensil safe.
Delicate foods such as eggs, fish and vegetables release best from a ceramic or PTFE non-stick surface. Look for our Gentle Frying range for effortless flipping and easy cleaning.
Pro+ pans add an extra performance layer — a more durable coating, a reinforced honeycomb structure or a heavier multi-ply base — along with an extended warranty compared with the standard Pro line.
Pre-heat the empty pan over medium heat, add oil and let it shimmer before adding food. Ingredients naturally release once a crust forms, so resist the urge to move them too early.
Every WMF frying pan is suitable for all hob types, including induction, as well as gas, ceramic and electric. Most are also oven-safe up to 250°C.

How to sear the perfect steak

Pre-heat the dry pan on medium-high until a drop of water dances across the surface. Add a little high-heat oil, lay the steak away from you and leave it untouched until a crust forms — it will release by itself when it is ready to turn.
